Ms. Boggs

"I like index cards, because if you write stuff down you can retain it."

This connection is supported in scholarly literature, as in
Umejima, et al. 2021.


Mr. Newton

"One tip I learned is taking flash cards to class. Instead of bringing a notebook to class and taking notes in there and then transposing those onto flash cards, I learned in grad school - 'oh, I'm just gonna take the flash cards' - as the teacher makes different points of emphasis 'thats being on a flash card, that one's being on a flash card' -- and then I don't have to spend the extra time making them."


Ms. Churchwell

"I like to go over my notes and then walk away from it for a little bit, maybe do something active, and then come back to it again. I think study breaks are important."
